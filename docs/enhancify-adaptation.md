# Enhancify Adaptation Notes

Enhancify is configured as a low-ticket, fast-cycle, phone-first sales motion. The coach should behave differently than a slower consultative or enterprise-style sales coach.

## Motion Differences

- Average contract value is roughly $1,500 per year, not a large enterprise deal.
- Leads are warm inbound, so the opening should quickly connect to stated interest instead of behaving like a cold outbound call.
- Most conversations are phone-only.
- The target is a one-call close when qualification, pain, economics, and expectations are clear.
- The full cycle is normally under two weeks, so the coach should avoid overbuilding multi-step strategy unless the call is genuinely blocked.
- The 30-minute call must cover discovery, quantification, solution, expectation-setting, and close.

## Coaching Bias

The live-call coach should optimize for:

- Fast Contractor A vs Contractor B identification.
- Quantifying pain with simple math before solutioning.
- Connecting the product to the prospect's current problem instead of listing features.
- Recommending the correct annual plan based on adoption/support need.
- Asking clearly for the business and handling the first no without becoming pushy.
- Disqualifying poor-fit opportunities instead of forcing a pitch.

## Enhancify-Specific Guardrails

The coach must not position Enhancify as a lender. It should describe Enhancify as a technology platform or marketplace connecting applicants with lending options.

The coach must preserve these distinctions:

- Soft-pull pre-approval is not final approval.
- Final approval requires the applicant to complete the lender process and usually involves a hard inquiry.
- The applicant/customer receives funds directly, not the contractor.
- Enhancify does not control rates, terms, amounts, approvals, underwriting, or funding timelines.
- 0% introductory options are credit-card marketplace options, not 0% loans.
- Typical funding timing must be qualified instead of guaranteed.

## Source Policy

The repo stores summarized structured data, not the full training scripts. Source references are tracked inside `config/companies/enhancify.json` under `sourceMetadata`.
