# Period Coaching Report Prompt

Create coaching reports from the lower-cadence summaries in this packet.

The output should respect this delivery model:

- Individual call reviews are not part of daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly PDFs. They belong in Slack messages or the frontend on-demand call review UI.
- Each rep receives one private summary for the period.
- The sales manager receives one team report with team-level trends and individual rep summaries.
- Coaching should focus on the highest-leverage behavior, not every possible flaw.
- Separate coaching leverage from compliance risk. Compliance belongs in the watchout/risk section unless it is severe enough to override the selling behavior focus.
- When choosing the focus, prefer upstream Decoded behaviors that create downstream improvement: qualification, quantification, discovery, solution-to-pain, close control, feature-dump control, then opening.

For each rep report, include:

- Overall period score and score trend when previous-period data exists.
- Strongest improvement or strength to reinforce.
- The single most important focus for the next period.
- Recurring compliance issues and exact watchouts.
- Any areas moving backward compared with previous periods.
- A concise action plan for the next period.

For the manager report, include:

- Team performance summary.
- Team score trend and movement versus previous period when available.
- Rep-by-rep coaching priorities.
- Recurring team compliance risks.
- Reps improving, declining, and needing manager attention.
- The one team coaching theme for the next period.

Do not include raw call IDs in user-facing text. Use company, prospect, or rep names when a specific example is needed.
